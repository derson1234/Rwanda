Main political parties and political forces

Rwandan Patriotic Front (RPF), Parti démocrate centriste (PDC), Parti libéral (PL), Parti social démocrate (PSD), Ideal Democratic Party (IDP). Opponents of the government include the externally based Rwanda National Congress (RNC), Forces démocratiques unifiées (FDU-Inking), partly based in Rwanda, and rebels based in the Democratic Republic of Congo, Forces démocratiques de libération du Rwanda (FDLR), which include the former Interahamwe, and members of the former Rwandan army (Forces armées rwandaises—FAR)

National legislature

National Assembly, with 80 members: 53 directly elected and 27 indirectly elected by representatives of special interest groups; Senate, with 24 members: 16 indirectly elected and eight appointed by the president .
